Frequently Asked Questions

Quick Answers

Find answers to common questions about our courses and services.

What courses do you offer?

We offer comprehensive driving courses (beginner to advanced, manual and automatic transmission) and computer courses (basic skills to advanced programming).

How long does it take to get a license?

Typically 4-6 weeks for beginners, depending on your schedule and learning pace. Advanced courses may take longer.

Do you provide vehicles for training?

Yes, we provide well-maintained training vehicles for both driving lessons and practical sessions.

What are your fees?

Our fees are competitive and vary by course type. Contact us for detailed pricing information and payment plans.

Do you offer job placement assistance?

Yes, we provide job placement assistance and connections with employers in the transport and IT sectors.
